Davao, Philippines

Davao City, located on the southern island of Mindanao in the Philippines, is known for its natural beauty, vibrant culture, and safety. It's the largest city in the country by land area and serves as a major economic and tourism hub in the region. Davao is famous for Mount Apo, the Philippines' tallest peak, as well as its rich biodiversity, including the Philippine eagle, the country's national bird. The city is also celebrated for its durian fruit, tropical beaches, and the Kadayawan Festival, an annual event honoring the city's indigenous heritage and bountiful harvests.
